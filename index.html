The Monk Diamond
The Diamond / Discovery / Exhibition / The House of Volkov

The Monk Diamond is one of the rarest and the most valuable diamonds in Europe. It has a
fascinating history. It was owned by the Russian nobilty, stolen by a famous criminal gang
and then found hidden in remote mountains in one of the most sensational discoveries of
the century. Find out more about the Monk Diamond in this website or come and see it for
yourself at the House of Volkov
