The Monk Diamond

The Monk Diamond is famous for its distinctive green colour. A Russain nobleman bought
the diamond for his wife in 1889, but it was stolen during the Russain Revlolution. For
the next 30 years, the Monk Diamond's whereabouts where unknown. In 1947 it was
discovered during a police raid on a gang of petty crimimals in Moscow. It was returned
to the nobleman's family. The nobleman's son decided that the Monk Diamond was
unlucky and sold it to the House of Volkov, Moscow's oldest jewelery house. The House of Volkov
paid an undiscolsed sum for the jewel, but it was rumered to be the most expensive
diamond sale ever.
